MISSING CELEBRITY IMAGES
========================

HOW TO ADD IMAGES:
==================

Option 1: Download from the internet
- Search for free celebrity images on sites like:
  * Unsplash (unsplash.com)
  * Pexels (pexels.com)
  * Pixabay (pixabay.com)
- Download images in JPG or PNG format
- Save them with the exact filenames listed above
- Place them in this folder: app/src/main/assets/celebrity_images/

Option 2: Use the download script
- Run: powershell -ExecutionPolicy Bypass -File download_celebrity_images.ps1
- The script will attempt to download missing images
